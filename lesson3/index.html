Site Name

The site name will be Hot Cold Weather and the url will be hotcoldweather.com.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help those who like hot weather find it and avoid hot weather and for those that like cold weather to find it and avoid hot weather. Along with that it will provide links to various locations or suggested activities for the desired weather. Along with the suggested links for activities there will also be a section for advertising various gear items to help them find and purchase any items that they may need to enjoy the weather and activity!
