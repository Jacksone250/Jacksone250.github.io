Font List

I will be using this font list in this order of precidence:

Roboto, Trebuchet MS, Lucida Sans Unicode, Lucida Grande, Lucida Sans, Arial, or sans-serif.

These fonts are of all similar typography and should any one of them fail as they may not be supported by the browser or a link might break users will not be greeted with a totally unfamiliar webpage.
