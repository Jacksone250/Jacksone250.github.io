Target Audience

My target Audience for Hot Cold Weather will be adults who like to spend time outside adventuring and are looking for the next place to backpack, white water raft, rock climb, or any of the other many outdoor activities. For example if a person was looking for a great area to camp in during the winter we would provide links and weather conditions in those areas for them to review.

Persona's

These Persona's will represent two of the various users we expect to see at our website most frequently. While they may not be the only people we see we will be catering to them the most as consider what content to advertise and links to add as well.

Persona 1

John is a 45 year old married man who likes to go hunting, fishing, and mountian bike riding. He sometimes takes his family along with him as well. He and his family like to go on weekend adventures during the summer with the kids and don't shy away from the winter activities as well. Thier annual household income is $80K, provding them with a moderate budget to spend on these activities. John grew up with his father who also enjoyed hunting and fishing with his children. While John isn't inexperienced with using verious forms of personal technology from big to small he prefers to do his work at a workstation where it is easier for him to see all of the information.

Persona 2

Jill is a 23 year old single woman who enjoys hiking, backpacking, and photographying wildlife. With a flexible work schedule she often has time for spontaneous activities outside. She is also apart of several outdoor grops that go white water rafting, climbiing, and sky diving. With her flexible schedule she is also able to travel further to explore other areas that are further away from her home. After graduating with a bachelors degree Jill makes $60K annually which she budgets to spend a large porption of on her favorite activities and has atleast one large trip a year internationally.

Senarios

  • Senario 1

    Jill got off work early on Thursday and has the Friday through Sunday off now. With about 8 hours till sun down she wanted to take the opportunity to find a fun place to camp nearby. While checking the weather at HotColdWeather.com she sees that there are several suggestions for hiking, climbing and camping near her. After looking through some camping areas she finds the perfect spot and heads out.

  • Senario 2

    With the kids out of school for the summer and a holiday weekend coming up John begins planning with his wife to take the kids out for a long weekend. As they brainstorm a few ideas they decide to look up the weather at a couple of different places to see if that will help them make a final decision. While looking up the weather in the area at HotColdWeather.com John notices a muntian biking trail that he hasn't done before and looks easy enough for the entire family to enjoy as well. Similarly, his wife finds a hike to a hot springs that she used to go to as a child with her parents. With a long weekend they'll be able to do both with the entire family.

  • Senario 3

    Aftering completing her goals for the day at work Jill has the rest of the afternoon off. Wanting to find a place to hike that will be near her she returns to HotColdWeather.com and looks up hikes in the area along with the weather report for their locations. With in short oreder she has found a hike with some nice weather that will allow her to take pictures in the evening.

  • Senario 4

    John has been looking forward to the start of deer season since it closed last year eager to get out on opening day he took work off in advance and spent a few weekends prior scouting several of his prefered hunting areas. Him and his hunting buddy decide after checking with HotColdWeather.com that the conditions at their favorite spot will be perfect and that they will startthere this year.

  • Senario 5

    The group Jill white water rafts with is planning a trip near her. As the planned trip nears she begins checking the weather where they will be rafting. Unfortuntely, there will be severe storms there that will make the situation to dangerous to raft in. Fortunately, since she was checking HotColdWeather.com it made several suggestions to other rivers that would be out of the effected area. After notifying the group of the storms and suggesting several other options that will be better they are able to enjoy their weekend.
